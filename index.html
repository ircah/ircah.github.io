IRC Against Humanity

Welcome to IRC Against Humanity, an IRC community for playing the popular game called Cards Against Humanity. You can join us in the ##ircah channel on the Libera IRC network (webchat).

Warning: This game may contain offensive language / jokes. If you're not comfortable with that, it's probably better to stay away.

Rules

  • Use common sense.
  • Be nice to others. Inside jokes are fine as long as all involved people are okay with it.
  • Do not spam. If you want to paste more than 3 lines, use a pastebin such as dpaste.com.
  • Do not advertise other channels.
  • Label NSFW links clearly, on the same line.
  • Do not play with clones.
